MENU 01

Consommé Carmen

Consommé flavored with tomato purée in beef stock and garnished with seeded  tomatoes, juliennes of sweet pimentos, boiled rice and chervil (France)

Poulet Sauté Chasseur

Hunter’s Chicken, crisp sautéed chicken served with a hearty sauce made with  tomatoes, mushrooms, a splash of white wine and brandy,  flavored with tarragon (France).

Pommes Loretta

Mashed potatoes combined with egg yolks, seasoned with butter, salt and  pepper and then deep fried in the shape of balls (France).

Haricots Verts

Blanched green beans sautéed in olive oil with garlic and salt (France).

MENU 02

Bisque D’écrevisse

Crayfish and vegetable soup made from tail meat of crayfish and vegetables  like tomatoes, onion, and celery; seasoned with sherry, salt and pepper; garnished with whipped cream (France).

Escalope De Veau viennoise

Flattened veal seasoned with flour, eggs and breadcrumbs; deep-fried and  served with lemon slices and steamed potatoes

Pommes Batailles

Potatoes diced and deep-fried; seasoned with salt (France).

Epinards au Gratin

Spinach steamed, seared and chopped steaks layered with cheese, rice andfresh cream and then baked (France).

MENU 03

Crème Du Barry

Cauliflower soup flavored with stock, potatoes and leeks; seasoned with salt,  pepper and garlic; garnished with chopped parsley while serving (France).

Darne De Saumon Grille

Marinated salmon slices (Olive oil, lemon and seasoning), grilled (France)

Sauce paloise

Derivative of Hollandaise (Béarnaise) sauce mixed with herbs, mint leaves and  shallots (France)

Pommes Fondant

Sliced potatoes fried in butter and cooked in chicken stock along with herbs and  seasoning (France).

Petits Pois A La Flamande

Green peas cooked in butter and chicken stock and then seasoned (France).

MENU 04

Veloute Dame Blanche

Velvety textures, pearl colour, smooth soup with a slight glaze. Served piping  hot, garnished with chicken quenelles – poached or fried. Rich taste of almond  and adequately seasoned (France).

Cote De Porc Charcuterie

Pork ribs browned and flavoured with onions, tomatoes, white wine and  mustard with seasoning (France).

Pommes De Terre A La Crème

Boiled potatoes cooked in milk, flavoured with butter and heavy cream;  seasoned and garnished with chopped parsley (France).

Carottes Glace Au Gingembre

Batons of carrots marinated with olive oil, garlic cloves, ginger root, cumin,  honey and sea salt; baked and served garnished with basil (France).

MENU 05

Cabbage Chowder

Cabbage, carrots and potatoes cooked in seasoned water and then combined  with milk and butter until finished (England). 

Poulet A La Rex

Chicken cooked in a white to blonde sauce along with juliennes of capsicum or  onion, seasoned with salt and pepper (France). 

Pommes Marguises

Mashed potatoes mixed with seasonings and egg yolks, baked till lightly  browned and served with cooked tomato concassé and chopped parsley  (France).

Ratatouille

Eggplant, Zucchini, Yellow squash and bell peppers thinly sliced, combined with  tomato concassé and tomato paste, seasoned with herbs, salt and pepper,  baked and served with mascarpone cheese.

MENU 06

Barquettes Assorti

A pastry shell shaped like a boat filled with fruit, vegetables, custard etc.  (France)

Stroganoff De Boeuf

Fried beef, mushrooms and shallots cooked in sour cream served on a bed of  rice, egg noodles, fettuccine or mashed potatoes (Russian).

Pommes Persilles

Sautéed and browned cubes of potatoes seasoned with salt and pepper,  combined with finely chopped garlic and parsley (France).

Riz Pilaf

Unwashed rice cooked in a flavourful broth along with spices and seasonings  (France).

MENU 07

Duchesse Nantua

Duchesse potatoes cooked in Nantua sauce comprising of Béchamel as base,  cream, crayfish tails and butter (France).

Poulet Maryland

Chicken breasts (boneless) marinated in buttermilk, coated with breadcrumbs,  eggs, and flour and seared in butter, drizzled with béchamel and garnished with  glazed bananas (France)

Croquette Potatoes

Mashed potatoes combined with milk, salt, pepper, beaten egg yolks and flour,  shaped into balls and then shallow fried (France)

Banana fritters

Mashed bananas combined with milk, eggs, baking powder, salt, margarine and  flour and then fried until golden brown (Jamaica)

Corn gallets

Corn meal cooked in milk, shaped flat and then fried on both sides (France).

MENU 08

Kromeskies

Bacon rolled and stuffed with pork/ lamb meat and then fried in batter made  with flour, salt and oil (Poland).

Filet De Sols Walweska

Sole fillets cooked in Mornay sauce and fumet and glazed in salamander.  Served with langoustine tails and slices of truffle (France)

Pommes Lyonnaise

Sliced pan-fried potatoes and thinly sliced onions, sautéed in butter with parsley  (France).

Funghi Marirati

Mushrooms combined with reduced water, oil, lemon juice, bay leaf, garlic,  peppercorns and salt (France).

MENU 09

Vol-Au-Vent De Volaille Et Jambon

Vol-au-vent stuffed with mushrooms cooked with ham, chicken and béchamel  (France).

Poulet a la kiev

Chicken Kiev is a dish of chicken fillet pounded and rolled around cold butter,  then coated with eggs and bread crumbs, and either fried or baked (Russia).

Creamy Mashed Potatoes

Mashed potatoes combined with butter, cream and milk and then seasoned  with salt and pepper (USA).

Butter tossed green peas

Green peas blanched and tossed in butter and seasoned with salt and pepper (USA).

MENU 10

Quiche Lorraine

Quiche stuffed with cream, bacon and cheese; eggs beaten with chopped onions and then baked (Germany)

Roast Lamb

Mince the garlic and rosemary leaves. Flip the lamb leg over again and rub the  top with the chopped garlic and rosemary. Cover the lamb loosely with foil. Put the lamb back in the oven and cook at 325°F for one hour (England).

Mint sauce 

Mint sauce is a sauce traditionally made from finely chopped peppermint  leaves, soaked in vinegar, and a small amount of sugar (Rome).

Pommes Parisienne

Potatoes scooped out with melon scooper and fried in clarified butter until golden brown (France)
